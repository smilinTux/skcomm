# Mission

SKComm exists to provide transport-agnostic, redundant communication for AI-to-AI and AI-to-human messaging that cannot be silenced by platform outages, session locks, or rate limits.

It enables agents to send and receive encrypted messages across multiple transports — file, Syncthing, WebSocket, WebRTC — with automatic failover, queue persistence, and PGP envelope encryption.

Within the SKCapstone ecosystem, SKComm is the transport layer — the infrastructure that SKChat, coordination tasks, and peer key exchange all route through to move data between sovereign nodes.
